❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 How do I allow MoveWithMii to access my health data?

iPhone Settings:

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Health
  4. Find MoveWithMii
  5. Turn on Steps, Active Energy, and Exercise Minutes

🚫 How do I enable app blocking?

When you first try to select apps to block, iOS will ask for permission. Simply tap "Allow" when prompted. If you missed it:

  1. Go to Settings > Screen Time
  2. Make sure Screen Time is enabled
  3. Restart MoveWithMii and try again

📊 Why aren't my steps updating?

Try these solutions:

  • Pull down on the main screen to refresh
  • Check Health app permissions (see above)
  • Make sure your iPhone is counting steps (check Health app)
  • Restart the app completely

🎯 How do I change my daily step goal?

Tap the target icon (🎯) in the top right corner of the main screen.

🔓 My apps aren't unlocking when I reach my goal

Try these steps:

  1. Force close and reopen MoveWithMii
  2. Pull down to refresh your step count
  3. If still stuck, restart your iPhone

💳 How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple:

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find MoveWithMii and tap it
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

🔄 How do I restore my Pro purchase?

In MoveWithMii:

  1. Go to Settings tab
  2. Tap Subscription
  3. Tap Restore Purchases
